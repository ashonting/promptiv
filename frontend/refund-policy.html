Refund Policy

Effective: June 2025

Thank you for subscribing to Promptiv. Your satisfaction matters to us. Please review our refund policy below.

Subscriptions

  • All Promptiv Pro plans are billed monthly via our payment partner, Paddle.
  • Subscriptions are non-refundable. Once a payment has been processed, it cannot be refunded, prorated, or credited, even if you cancel before the end of your billing period.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Cancellation will prevent future charges but does not trigger a refund for the current billing period.

Exceptional Circumstances

If you believe you have been charged in error or encounter a technical problem that prevents use of Promptiv, please contact our support team. We may review refund requests on a case-by-case basis at our discretion.

Contact

For all billing or refund questions, email support@promptiv.io.
