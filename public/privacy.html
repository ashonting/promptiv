Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-25.

What we collect

If you sign up to be notified about Promptiv, we collect:

  • The email address you submit
  • A keyed (HMAC-SHA-256) hash of your IP, used only to dedupe duplicate signups
  • The referrer URL if your browser sends one
  • Optional answers you provide on the post-signup screen (budget bucket, home airport, biggest travel frustration)

What we don't collect

No cookies. No third-party analytics. No tracking pixels. No payment information.

How we use it

To email you when there is a real product to share. To learn what kind of trips matter most to people considering Promptiv. We don't sell or share your data.

Deletion

Email adam@distillworks.com and ask us to delete your signup. We will do so within 7 days and reply to confirm.
